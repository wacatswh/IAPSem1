Granblue Fantasy is a role-playing video game developed by Cygames. The game plays as a role-playing video game with turn-based battles. The game also contains summons and a class system that alters the main character's move-set and growth. Characters gain levels and abilities by acquiring experience.



The gameplay is centred upon building squadrons composed of individual characters represented as cards with different attributes, and then sending said squadrons out on missions. The player takes on the role of an admiral and organises their fleets in battle in order to win.



In Clash Royale, playes are ranked by level and trophies. A player wins a battle by destroying more towers than the opponent, or by destroying the opponent's "King's Tower", which grants and automatic three "crown" victory. Player uses cards drawn from the deck to destroy the opponent's towers in order to win the game.
